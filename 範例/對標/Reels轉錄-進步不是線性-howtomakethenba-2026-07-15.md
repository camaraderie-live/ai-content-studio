## 逐字稿（原文）

Progress isn't linear. There are always going to be times when things don't go as well as you hoped for, times when you feel like the work you're putting in isn't being rewarded. But timing is a big part of success, and a lot of times, it's out of your control. You have to be patient, and most importantly, never stop working. Try not to let yourself spiral into negative thoughts, and if you find yourself doing so, find a way to pull yourself out. For me, that's being in the lab, working on my game, and being in the weight room, working on my body. I'm not perfect, but when I find myself stressed, I think about the things I'm grateful for, that it's good to be alive and well, to even be in the position I'm in. Remember, it's a privilege for us to work towards our goals.
